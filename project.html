Citizen Feedback Portal & SDGs

A Citizen Feedback Portal plays an essential role in improving governance and ensuring public services meet citizens' needs. When integrating it with SDGs, targets, and indicators, it becomes an effective tool for assessing and aligning public policies with global sustainability frameworks.

SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

Target: 16.7 - Ensure responsive, inclusive, participatory, and representative decision-making at all levels.

Indicator: 16.7.1 - Proportion of population who believe decision-making is inclusive and responsive, by sex, age, disability, and population group.

  • Example: Citizen feedback portals can enable the government to understand whether citizens feel their input influences policy.
  • Advantages: Improved transparency, trust, and accountability in governance.
