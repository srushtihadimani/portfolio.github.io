Project Overview

Welcome to the Aurangabad Smart City Project! Our goal is to create a sustainable and technology-driven city that improves the quality of life for its citizens, promotes innovation, and enhances urban efficiency.

Problem Space and How It Was Arrived At

Aurangabad's Smart City initiative aims to tackle the urban challenges of infrastructure inefficiency, traffic congestion, environmental sustainability, and access to essential services. Through data-driven insights and innovative solutions, we aim to improve urban living.

The challenges identified are closely aligned with issues like traffic management, waste management, public transport, sustainable energy, and healthcare. Our approach integrates advanced technology and human-centered design to ensure sustainability and efficiency in every aspect of the city.

Key Findings:

  • Optimized traffic and transport systems
  • Pollution-free urban environment
  • Smart city technologies for better governance and services

Future Vision: By focusing on sustainability, connectivity, and quality of life, Aurangabad will emerge as a model for smart cities across the world.
