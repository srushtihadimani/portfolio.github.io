Aurangabad Smart City Project

Aurangabad, our smart city project, was conceptualized to create a city that is not just infrastructure-efficient but also functionally smart. The goal was to ensure seamless functioning, improve the quality of life, and generate sustainable revenue streams.

The project was inspired by insights from the white paper authored by Prakash Hegde and addressed challenges related to traffic management, tourism, food production, public transport, technology, and community well-being.

Key Findings from the White Paper

  • Urban Dynamics and Street Life: Inspired by Mumbai’s vibrant street life, Aurangabad will incorporate art, culture, and public spaces reflecting the residents' dynamic urban life.
  • City Design and Smart Cities: Drawing lessons from Matheran’s vehicle-free model, Aurangabad will prioritize sustainability and cultural heritage over mere technological advancements.
  • Commuting and City Connectivity: Inspired by Mumbai’s local trains, Aurangabad will focus on efficient and eco-friendly public transport systems.

Business Cases for Aurangabad Smart City

  • Optimal Road Network Design
  • Smart Public Transportation Scheduling
  • Waste and Water Management System
  • Interactive City Entertainment Map
  • Holographic Live Streaming
  • Citizen Feedback Portal (Smart Governance)
  • Real-Time Disaster Response System

Problem Definition

Aurangabad aims to tackle urban challenges by focusing on efficient infrastructure, sustainability, and economic viability. Key aspects include optimizing traffic management, integrating smart public transport, enhancing the flow of goods and people, and preserving ecological balance.

The city combines human-centered design with ecological preservation and sustainable growth through tech parks, logistics hubs, tourism, and leveraging its coastal location for ports.
