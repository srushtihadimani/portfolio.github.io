Problem Statement

Aurangabad, a city known for its rich cultural heritage, is facing challenges in managing urban infrastructure, optimizing transportation, improving sustainability, and increasing citizen engagement. The city lacks an integrated approach to address issues like traffic congestion, inefficient public transportation, waste management, and overall citizen governance. There is a need for intelligent, data-driven solutions that promote sustainable growth, enhance quality of life, and streamline services.

Findings from White Paper

The findings highlight the city's issues related to urban planning, the lack of a unified traffic management system, insufficient public transportation, and ineffective waste management practices. The paper also emphasized the need for sustainable development, the integration of smart technologies, and citizen-centric policies to create a smarter, greener city.

Problem Definition

Aurangabad aims to tackle urban challenges by focusing on efficient infrastructure, sustainability, and economic viability. Key aspects include optimizing traffic management, integrating smart public transport, enhancing the flow of goods and people, and preserving ecological balance.

The city combines human-centered design with ecological preservation and sustainable growth through tech parks, logistics hubs, tourism, and leveraging its coastal location for ports.

Business Cases for Aurangabad Smart City

  • Optimal Road Network Design

    By utilizing data analytics and AI algorithms, the road network design will improve traffic flow and reduce congestion. Dynamic traffic signals and automated rerouting will optimize traffic management across the city.

  • Smart Public Transportation Scheduling

    Leveraging IoT and machine learning, the transportation system will predict traffic patterns and adjust schedules in real-time, ensuring minimal delays.

  • Waste and Water Management System

    Smart sensors and IoT will optimize waste collection and water usage. Real-time data will detect leaks and ensure sustainable resource management.

  • Interactive City Entertainment Map

    AR technology will create an interactive map, guiding residents and visitors to nearby cultural events, restaurants, and entertainment options in real-time.

  • Holographic Live Streaming

    Holographic technology will enable 3D viewing of events, creating an immersive experience for residents and visitors alike.

  • Citizen Feedback Portal (Smart Governance)

    This portal will enable citizens to actively participate in governance, providing real-time feedback on city services and infrastructure.

  • Real-Time Disaster Response System

    AI-driven systems will predict and respond to disasters in real-time, deploying emergency services and assisting in recovery efforts.
