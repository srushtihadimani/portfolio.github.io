Adjacency List Representation

Purpose: Efficiently stores the graph (road network) as a list of lists, where each node has a list of adjacent nodes.

Application: Used to represent roads and intersections in the city's traffic management system.

Kruskal's Algorithm (MST Construction)

Purpose: Builds a Minimum Spanning Tree (MST) by connecting all nodes with the least possible total edge weight.

Application: Used for designing the least-cost road network in the city, minimizing infrastructure costs.
