Course Overview

The Design and Analysis of Algorithms course focuses on the creation and optimization of algorithms used to solve problems efficiently. The course provides foundational knowledge and practical experience in designing algorithms for various types of problems, analyzing their efficiency, and understanding their limitations.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Sorting Algorithms (Bubble Sort, Merge Sort, Quick Sort)
  • Searching Algorithms (Linear Search, Binary Search)
  • Greedy Algorithms and their Applications
  • Divide and Conquer Strategies
  • Graph Algorithms (DFS, BFS, Shortest Path)
  • Dynamic Programming and Backtracking
  • Algorithm Complexity Analysis (Big O Notation)

This course is designed for individuals looking to strengthen their problem-solving skills through algorithmic thinking. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, you'll develop essential skills to approach and solve complex computational problems.
