What is the Course About?

This course delves deep into the design and analysis of algorithms, exploring foundational topics like recursion, time and space complexity, and advanced structures such as Red-Black Trees and Segment Trees. Real-world case studies, including the Travelling Salesperson Problem and optimization problems, are covered to bridge theory and application.
