About the Course

Dive into the core principles of algorithms and data structures, learning how they power modern computing. This course introduces you to:

  • Algorithm Fundamentals: Time and space complexities, recursion, and problem-solving strategies.
  • Advanced Topics: AVL Trees, Segment Trees, Graph Algorithms, and Optimization Techniques.
  • Real-World Applications: From smart city designs to real-time decision-making in logistics.

Why Study Algorithms?

Algorithms shape the world we live in, from powering search engines to managing transportation systems. This course equips you with the knowledge to:

  • Analyze and design efficient solutions to real-world problems.
  • Excel in competitive programming and technical interviews.
  • Bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and practical applications.
