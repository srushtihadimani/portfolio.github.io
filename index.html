Welcome to the Design and Analysis of Algorithms

Your gateway to mastering the logic and efficiency behind modern computing.

What is this course about?

This course dives deep into the fascinating world of algorithms and data structures. Whether you are new to the subject or want to expand your expertise, we focus on teaching you how to design, analyze, and implement efficient algorithms for solving complex problems.

Starting with foundational concepts such as time and space complexities, we move into advanced topics including graph algorithms, optimization techniques, and real-world applications.

Why study algorithms?

Algorithms are at the core of every major technology we use today, from search engines to transportation systems. This course empowers you with the skills to:

  • Understand and evaluate the efficiency of solutions using mathematical foundations.
  • Analyze real-world problems and develop practical, efficient algorithms.
  • Excel in competitive programming and prepare for coding interviews.
  • Design scalable systems and software for future challenges.

What will you learn?

By the end of the course, you’ll have mastered topics such as:

  • Core Concepts: Time complexity, recursion, backtracking.
  • Advanced Data Structures: AVL Trees, Segment Trees, Red-Black Trees.
  • Graph Algorithms: BFS, DFS, Dijkstra’s, and Minimum Spanning Trees.
  • Optimization: Knapsack Problem, Union-Find, and Huffman Coding.
  • Applications: Sorting large datasets, smart traffic systems, and resource allocation.
