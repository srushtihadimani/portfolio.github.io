The **Design and Analysis of Algorithms** course provides a comprehensive foundation in algorithms, data structures, and computational thinking. Explore theoretical knowledge and practical applications that empower you to tackle real-world problems with efficiency and precision.

What This Course Covers

This course takes you on a journey through:

  • Understanding the basics of algorithm efficiency, time complexity, and space analysis.
  • Learning fundamental and advanced data structures like arrays, linked lists, binary trees, and tries.
  • Mastering various sorting and searching algorithms, including Quick Sort and Binary Search.
  • Exploring real-world graph algorithms such as Dijkstra's and Prim's algorithms for optimization problems.
  • Discovering advanced concepts like NP-completeness and limitations of algorithmic power.
