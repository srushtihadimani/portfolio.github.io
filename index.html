Welcome to the Design and Analysis of Algorithms Course

This course is designed to provide students with an in-depth understanding of algorithms and their analysis. The key objective is to equip students with the knowledge and skills to design and analyze efficient algorithms for solving complex problems in computer science.

The course covers a wide range of topics, including:

  • Introduction to Algorithms
  • Sorting and Searching Algorithms
  • Divide and Conquer Strategies
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Graph Algorithms
  • Complexity Analysis and Big-O Notation

By the end of the course, students will have the practical skills and theoretical knowledge to approach complex problems in programming and software development.
