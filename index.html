About the Course

This course is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of the key concepts in algorithm design and analysis. Topics covered include:

  • Sorting and Searching Algorithms
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Divide and Conquer
  • Graph Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Complexity Analysis

By the end of this course, you'll be equipped with the skills to solve computational problems effectively and efficiently.
